#### 后缀介绍

**+**Denotes a lead(Pb)-free/RoHS-compliant package. +表示符合无铅(Pb)/RoHS标准的包装。

**T = Tape and reel.** T代表元器件包装方式为卷带装

**T&R = Tape and reel.** 同上

**TRL = Tape and reel** 同上

**00E** or **T0E** indicates IC supports EZ performance model only. No custom battery characterization. 00E或T0E表示IC仅支持EZ性能型号。不支持自定义电池特性。

**/V** denotes an automotive qualified part. /V表示合格的汽车零部件。

**\#** Denotes a RoHS-compliant device that may include lead(Pb) that is exempt under RoHS requirements. The lead finish is JESD97 category e3, and is compatible with both lead-based and lead-free soldering processes. # 指符合RoHS标准的设备，可能含有铅(Pb)。根据RoHS的要求，该产品可免于使用。铅的表面处理是 JESD97类别e3，兼容含铅和无铅焊接工艺。
